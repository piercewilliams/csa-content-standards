# Apple News

Platform: Apple News | §10.2

[⬇ Download this section](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/piercewilliams/csa-content-standards/main/docs/platform-apple-news.md){: .btn }

---

> **Where this platform overrides General Guidelines:** The headline character range is <span style="color: #dc2626;">**90–120 characters**</span> — substantially above the 80–100 universal default; performance data shows rising engagement through 110–119. <span style="color: #dc2626;">**Question headlines and number-led headlines underperform in organic algorithmic reach**</span> and should be avoided for that goal (a strategic exception for human-curated featured placement is documented below). <span style="color: #dc2626;">**AI disclosure requirements exceed General Guidelines §1.6**</span> — AI-generated or AI-assisted content requires a byline or co-byline and must be tagged in News Publisher metadata; AI-generated media must be clearly labeled. The <span style="color: #dc2626;">**subtitle field is a required publishing step**</span> with no General Guidelines equivalent. <span style="color: #dc2626;">**Thumbnail specs**</span> (300×300px minimum, 1:2 to 3:1 aspect ratio) have no General Guidelines equivalent.

---

### Platform Overview

Apple News delivers content natively across iPhone, iPad, and Mac via a combination of algorithmic personalization, social signals, and human editorial curation. Two publishing format options are available:

- **RSS feed** — standard layout; simpler setup
- **Apple News Format (ANF)** — <span style="color: #dc2626;">**recommended**</span>; JSON-based format with full typography control, photo galleries, parallax scrolling, and animations; renders natively and optimally across all Apple devices

Apple's human editorial team curates featured content. Quality, originality, and factual accuracy are prerequisites for featured placement — not just best practices.

### Headline
**(REQUIRED)**

<span style="color: #dc2626;">**Character count: 90–120 characters. Performance data shows engagement rising through 110–119 characters.**</span>

- <span style="color: #dc2626;">**Compelling and informative** — Apple News readers expect content that earns their full attention, not just a click</span>
- <span style="color: #dc2626;">**Write a separate Apple News title** — distinct from the shorter SEO/Discover variant; the Apple News title can be set as an override in News Publisher</span>
- <span style="color: #dc2626;">**Avoid question headlines for organic algorithmic reach** — they underperform in organic views</span>
- <span style="color: #dc2626;">**Avoid number-led headlines** — underperform on Apple News despite performing well on other platforms (including SmartNews)</span>
- Casing: follow destination site style guide

> **Featured placement exception:** Apple's human editorial team *favors* question and "What to Know" formats for editorially curated featured slots. If writing specifically to pursue a featured slot — not for broad algorithmic reach — question framing may help with human curators. These are separate optimization goals; choose the approach based on the specific objective for the piece.

### Subtitle (Excerpt)
**(REQUIRED)**

<span style="color: #dc2626;">**Populate the subtitle field in News Publisher.** Without a subtitle, Apple pulls from the first paragraph of the article body as the excerpt — that paragraph is rarely optimized as a standalone excerpt.</span>

- The subtitle appears below the headline in Apple News feeds
- Write it as a 1–2 sentence excerpt that complements the headline and earns the tap
- It should not repeat the headline verbatim

### Tone
**(REQUIRED)**

Polished, confident, editorially rich, trustworthy.

Apple News readers expect a curated, magazine-quality experience — not content that feels mass-produced. Editorial investment is visible in this environment. Content that reads as rushed or formulaic underperforms against the quality bar Apple's algorithm and editorial team reward.

**Target persona: The Apple News Explorer** — A cross-device reader who encounters content through algorithmic personalization, social signals, and human editorial curation. Expects polished visuals and content that feels worth reading in full. Core Drive: *Surface the best of what I care about and make it worth my time to read the whole thing.*

### Article Structure
**(REQUIRED)**

- Lean into **Apple News Format (ANF)** over basic RSS — ANF unlocks typography control, galleries, parallax, and animations that reward editorial investment and drive significantly higher engagement
- Maintain a **consistent publishing schedule** — the algorithm rewards regularity alongside engagement signals
- Select appropriate **content categories** (technology, lifestyle, sports, etc.) in News Publisher for discoverability
- Include **CTA boxes** within articles where appropriate — drive readers to newsletters, subscriptions, or owned properties
- **Sports content** has the strongest topic signal on Apple News. Business and lifestyle content consistently perform lower — compensate with stronger headline execution in those verticals

### Image Requirements
**(REQUIRED)**

| Spec | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Lead image minimum width | 1,200px (cross-platform baseline) |
| Thumbnail minimum dimensions | <span style="color: #dc2626;">**300×300px**</span> |
| Thumbnail aspect ratio | <span style="color: #dc2626;">**1:2 to 3:1**</span> |
| Image resolution | High-resolution encouraged throughout |
| Galleries | Supported via ANF |
| Embedded video | Supported (YouTube/Vimeo) |
| AI-generated or AI-augmented media | <span style="color: #dc2626;">**Must be clearly labeled**</span> |

### AI Content Policy
**(REQUIRED)**

<span style="color: #dc2626;">Apple imposes disclosure requirements beyond General Guidelines §1.6. Apply all of the following in addition to the standard AI disclaimer.</span>

- <span style="color: #dc2626;">**Articles generated by or assisted by AI** must include a byline or co-byline and be marked as AI-generated in News Publisher metadata</span>
- **AI used as a tool** for idea generation, headline generation, research, or editing does not require disclosure if a journalist composes and vets the final article
- <span style="color: #dc2626;">**AI-generated images, video, and audio** must be clearly labeled per publication standards</span>
- <span style="color: #dc2626;">**Include an explanation of how AI was used** within the article to maintain reader trust</span>

### Discoverability

Apple News surfaces content through a combination of three signals:

1. **Algorithmic personalization** — based on user interaction patterns, topic relevance, and social engagement
2. **Social signals** — shares and engagement within the Apple News ecosystem
3. **Human editorial curation** — Apple's editorial team selects featured content; quality, originality, and factual accuracy are prerequisites

**Promote feature:** Publishers can submit up to 6 articles per section for promoted placement.

Consistent publishing cadence is rewarded — irregular publication depresses algorithmic standing over time.

### Monetization

- Ads managed via **Workbench**
- Publishers keep **100% of self-sold ad revenue**; **70% of Apple-sold revenue**
- **Affiliate content is algorithmically prioritized** — product reviews and gear guides that already exist for other channels can be repurposed for Apple News with minimal additional production; strong alignment for Experiences and Everyday Living verticals
- **CTA boxes** supported within articles — drive readers to newsletters, subscriptions, or owned properties

### Analytics

Apple News analytics are available in **News Publisher**. Key metrics: views, engagement, shares.

Topic performance signal for planning:
- **Sports:** strongest topic signal
- **Business and lifestyle:** consistently lower performance — compensate with stronger headline execution

### What to Avoid

| Prohibited | Reason |
|---|---|
| <span style="color: #dc2626;">Question headlines (for algorithmic reach)</span> | <span style="color: #dc2626;">Underperform in organic Apple News views — see Featured Placement exception in Headline section</span> |
| <span style="color: #dc2626;">Number-led headlines</span> | <span style="color: #dc2626;">Underperform on Apple News despite performing well on other platforms</span> |
| <span style="color: #dc2626;">Omitting the subtitle field</span> | <span style="color: #dc2626;">Apple pulls from first paragraph if no subtitle; first paragraphs are rarely excerpt-optimized</span> |
| <span style="color: #dc2626;">Thumbnail below 300×300px or outside 1:2–3:1 ratio</span> | <span style="color: #dc2626;">Platform image spec</span> |
| <span style="color: #dc2626;">Publishing AI-generated content without byline/co-byline</span> | <span style="color: #dc2626;">Apple AI content policy — required; also tag in News Publisher metadata</span> |
| <span style="color: #dc2626;">Unlabeled AI-generated images, video, or audio</span> | <span style="color: #dc2626;">Apple AI content policy</span> |
| Using RSS-only when ANF is available | ANF delivers significantly higher engagement via rich media support |
| Irregular publishing cadence | Algorithm rewards consistent publication alongside engagement signals |

### Pre-Publish Checklist

- Apple News title: 90–120 characters (110–119 target), compelling and informative, distinct from SEO/Discover headline
- No question headlines (unless specifically targeting featured placement — see Headline section)
- No number-led headlines
- Subtitle field populated in News Publisher
- Content categories selected for discoverability
- Publishing format: ANF (preferred) or RSS
- Lead image: 1,200px+ wide
- Thumbnail: 300×300px minimum, aspect ratio 1:2 to 3:1
- All AI-generated or AI-augmented media clearly labeled
- If AI-generated or AI-assisted content: byline/co-byline present; tagged as AI-generated in News Publisher metadata; explanation of AI use included in article
- Standard AI disclaimer per General Guidelines §1.6 applied
- CTA box included where appropriate
- Promote feature submission considered (up to 6 articles per section)
