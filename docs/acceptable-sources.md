# 4. Acceptable Sources

> **Scope:** Defines approved sources for McClatchy content. All facts must be verified and all links must point to sources that meet these standards.

[⬇ Download this section](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/piercewilliams/csa-content-standards/main/docs/acceptable-sources.md){: .btn }

---

## Health & Wellness

| Source | URL | Type | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Mayo Clinic | [mayoclinic.org](https://www.mayoclinic.org) | Medical/Nonprofit | Evidence-based health info; widely cited for symptoms, conditions, treatments. Great for service articles on conditions & prevention. |
| CDC | [cdc.gov](https://www.cdc.gov) | Government | Official U.S. public health data, guidelines, seasonal health alerts. Seasonal content hooks (flu, allergies, heat safety). |
| NIH / MedlinePlus | [medlineplus.gov](https://medlineplus.gov) | Government | Plain-language health encyclopedia backed by NIH research. Good for explainers and FAQ-style content. |
| Harvard Health Publishing | [health.harvard.edu](https://www.health.harvard.edu) | Academic | Expert-reviewed wellness articles; strong on nutrition, mental health, aging. Often ranks well for health queries. |
| NAMI | [nami.org](https://www.nami.org) | Nonprofit | Mental health conditions, treatment options, support resources. Essential for mental health service content. |
| WHO | [who.int](https://www.who.int) | International Govt | Global health data, disease info, public health guidelines. Useful for broader context and global stats. |
| Cleveland Clinic Health Essentials | [health.clevelandclinic.org](https://health.clevelandclinic.org) | Medical/Nonprofit | Consumer-friendly health articles from top-ranked hospital system. Good model for tone in service health journalism. |
| Psychology Today | [psychologytoday.com](https://www.psychologytoday.com) | Media/Expert | Therapist directory and accessible mental health articles. Helpful for sourcing expert quotes and trends. |

## Home / DIY / Gardening

| Source | URL | Type | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| This Old House | [thisoldhouse.com](https://www.thisoldhouse.com) | Media | Expert-backed home improvement guides, how-tos, product reviews. Strong SEO presence; good for DIY content benchmarking. |
| USDA Plant Hardiness Zone Map | [planthardiness.ars.usda.gov](https://planthardiness.ars.usda.gov) | Government | Official planting zone data for regional gardening guidance. Essential for localized gardening calendars. |
| Cooperative Extension System (USDA) | [nifa.usda.gov](https://www.nifa.usda.gov/about-nifa/how-we-work/extension) | Government/Academic | University-backed local gardening, pest control, and home science resources. Hyper-local angle for regional publications. |
| National Association of Realtors (NAR) | [nar.realtor](https://www.nar.realtor/research-and-statistics) | Industry Assoc. | Housing market data, homebuyer trends, affordability stats. Key source for home-buying service content. |
| EPA Indoor Air Quality | [epa.gov](https://www.epa.gov/indoor-air-quality-iaq) | Government | Guidance on mold, radon, VOCs, and healthy home environments. Great for seasonal home safety articles. |
| The Spruce | [thespruce.com](https://www.thespruce.com) | Media | Broad home, garden, and decor coverage; Dotdash Meredith brand. Competitive benchmark for Everyday Living vertical. |
| Houzz | [houzz.com](https://www.houzz.com) | Platform/Media | Design inspiration, contractor directory, renovation cost data. Good for decor trend pieces and renovation budgets. |

## Personal Finance / Money

| Source | URL | Type | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) | [consumerfinance.gov](https://www.consumerfinance.gov) | Government | Consumer finance guides, complaint data, regulatory info. Authoritative for credit, mortgages, student loans. |
| Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED) | [fred.stlouisfed.org](https://fred.stlouisfed.org) | Government | Economic indicators, interest rates, inflation data. Useful for data-driven money content. |
| IRS.gov | [irs.gov](https://www.irs.gov) | Government | Tax deadlines, forms, credits/deductions explainers. Must-have for tax season content calendar. |
| NerdWallet | [nerdwallet.com](https://www.nerdwallet.com) | Media/Fintech | Credit cards, banking, investing guides; strong SEO competitor. Key benchmark; great for understanding what ranks. |
| Bankrate | [bankrate.com](https://www.bankrate.com) | Media | Rate data, calculators, personal finance guides. Competitor and data source for rate-related content. |
| Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) | [bls.gov](https://www.bls.gov) | Government | Employment, wages, CPI/inflation, cost of living data. Strong for jobs and cost-of-living articles. |
| Social Security Administration | [ssa.gov](https://www.ssa.gov) | Government | Benefits info, retirement planning, disability resources. Key for retirement and benefits service content. |

## Food & Recipes

| Source | URL | Type | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| USDA FoodData Central | [fdc.nal.usda.gov](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov) | Government | Nutrition data for any food item; official U.S. source. Back up nutrition claims in food/recipe content. |
| FDA Food Safety | [fda.gov/food](https://www.fda.gov/food) | Government | Recalls, food safety guidelines, labeling rules. Essential for food safety and recall coverage. |
| Serious Eats | [seriouseats.com](https://www.seriouseats.com) | Media | Science-driven recipes and technique explainers. Competitive benchmark for food content quality. |
| Bon Appétit | [bonappetit.com](https://www.bonappetit.com) | Media | Trend-forward recipes, restaurant culture, food trends. Good for food trend sourcing. |
| Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics | [eatright.org](https://www.eatright.org) | Professional Assoc. | Evidence-based nutrition guidance, dietitian resources. Authoritative for nutrition claims and expert sourcing. |

## General News & Reporting

| Source | URL | Type | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|

| Data.gov | [data.gov](https://data.gov) | Government | Open data catalog for U.S. government datasets. Great for data journalism and investigative pieces. |
